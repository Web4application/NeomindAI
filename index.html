NeomindAI System Overview

Abstract

This paper presents the architecture of NeomindAI, a hybrid system integrating Arduino sensors and actuators with advanced AI layers for data processing, predictive modeling, and physical interaction. The framework demonstrates a closed-loop approach where real-world signals inform AI decision-making, which then executes precise actions in the physical environment.

System Architecture

The system is structured as a pipeline:

[Arduino Sensors & Actuators] - Motors, LEDs, Cameras, LiDAR, Environmental Sensors ↓ [EDQ AI Layer] - Data Cleaning, Aggregation, Real-Time Analytics ↓ [SERAI AI Layer] - Advanced Reasoning, Predictive Modeling, Simulations ↓ [Arduino Execution] - Physical Actions, Feedback Loops ↓ [Real World]

Mathematical Modeling

Discussion

The modular AI layers (EDQ & SERAI) allow seamless integration of analytics, reasoning, and simulation. The feedback loop ensures continuous adaptation to real-world changes, making the system robust for robotics, IoT, and automation research.

Conclusion

NeomindAI provides a versatile framework for integrating physical devices with advanced AI reasoning, enabling real-time, intelligent control of actuators and sensors with predictive insights.
